## 2. ABOUT US

**URL:** https://www.naval-acad.bg/university/about-us/
**Page Title:** За нас -- Nikola Vaptsarov Naval Academy

### Content

#### Heading: За нас -- ВВМУ "Н.Й.Вапцаров"

The institution describes itself as Bulgaria's oldest technical educational establishment. The academy traces maritime education traditions to 1881, when the Naval School opened as the country's first technical institution for training engineers and sailors.

#### Key Historical Milestones
- **1881:** Naval School opened as Bulgaria's first technical institution
- **1904:** Became Machine School of the Fleet
- **1942:** Transformed into Royal Naval School with advanced navigation and technical departments
- **1949:** Renamed People's Naval School "Nikola Yonkov Vaptsarov"
- **1956:** Granted status as higher engineering maritime institution
- **1991:** Current name adopted

The academy possesses "необходимата материална база, включително уникални тренажори" (necessary material base including unique simulators) and qualified instructors. Curricula comply with international requirements for preparing naval officers and maritime industry specialists.

## 4. TRADITIONS AND MISSION

**URL:** https://www.naval-acad.bg/university/traditions-and-mission
**Page Title:** Традиция и мисия -- Nikola Vaptsarov Naval Academy

### Traditions Tab

The naval school has rich traditions dating to the 19th century, combining "теоретичното обучение с практиката" (theoretical instruction with practice), discipline, teamwork, and maritime culture. Core principles include:
- Institution for maritime education
- Training commanders for military and civilian fleets
- Integration of theory with practice
- Center for maritime education and science in Bulgaria
- Adherence to maritime training standards

### Mission Tab

NVNA prepares "висококвалифицирани специалисти и лидери" (highly qualified specialists and leaders) for Bulgarian armed forces, national security, and the economy. Activities include:
- Bachelor's degree programs in "Военно дело" (Military Affairs)
- Master's and doctoral programs
- Professional development training
- Scientific research and innovation
- Publishing and cultural activities

The planetarium is described as "one of the impressive attractions of Varna city." Sailors have historically revered the starry sky as their "infallible guide in the vastness of the world ocean."

- **Manufacturer:** Carl Zeiss (Jena, Germany)
- **Official Opening:** 1985
- **Star Hall Diameter:** 18 meters
- **Seating Capacity:** 120 seats
